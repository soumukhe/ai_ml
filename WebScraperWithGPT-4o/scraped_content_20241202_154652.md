# Prompt Engineering Guide

## On This Page...

Prompt engineering is a relatively new discipline for developing and optimizing prompts to efficiently use language models (LMs) for a wide variety of tasks. Researchers use prompt engineering to improve the capacity of LLMs on a wide range of common and complex tasks such as question answering and arithmetic. Prompt engineering is not just about designing and developing prompts. It encompasses a wide range of skills and techniques that are useful for interacting with LLMs.

Motivated by the high interest in developing with LLMs, we have created this new prompt engineering guide that contains all the latest papers, advanced techniques, and best practices.
